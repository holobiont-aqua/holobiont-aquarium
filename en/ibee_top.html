Activities
  1. Research on symbiotic environmental management technology
    Analysis of microbial community dynamics in closed circulation systems; development of ecosystem design methods based on heterotrophic bacteria
  2. Demonstration of ecosystem design in aquatic and terrestrial environments
    Construction and long-term operation of biological symbiosis systems in freshwater and saltwater environments; verification of plant cultivation using substrate design and carbon source supplementation
  3. Preparation and publication of technical documentation
    Development and public release of technical documents based on empirical data; systematization of fundamental knowledge on ecosystem management
